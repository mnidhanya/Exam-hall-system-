How Pedermed Works

📱

1. Surplus Detected

Food providers report surplus meals through our app or IoT sensors

🤖

2. AI Matching

Our algorithm matches surplus with nearby NGOs in real-time

📦

3. Notification

The System Notifies the nearby NGOs

❤️

4. Contact and Location

The Contact details and Location Details are shared with the NGOs

AI Demand Prediction

IoT Monitoring

Real-time surplus tracking with smart sensors and temperature monitoring

Multi-Language Support
